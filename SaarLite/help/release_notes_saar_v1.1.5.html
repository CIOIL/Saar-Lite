Installing the Patch

Needed manual installations via DA (types etc) and special instructions

  • Backup Saar Lite & NessDctmRest folders.

  • Stop Tomcat Service.

  • Delete NessDctmRest & SaarLite Folders.

  • Delete NessDctmRest SaarLite WAR Files If exist.

  • Delete Tomcat Cash.

  • Copy NessDctmRest.war & SaarLite.war.

  • Start Tomcat Service.

  • After Tomcat service had started Remove NessDctmRest.war, SaarLite.war.
